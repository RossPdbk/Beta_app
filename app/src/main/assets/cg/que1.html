Q1. Explain diffrence between Random Scan and Raster Scan ?

Ans:
Sr.No. Random Scan Raster Scan
1 . (Electron beam) Random scan operate by directing the
electron beam to only those parts of the
screen where the picture is to be drawn		 The electron beam starts at the left corner of the screen and
moves horizontally to the right
2 . (Use) Creation of diagrams using random scan
becomes easier,so can be used in engineering and scientific drawings.		 Raster graphics can be used in animations
3. (Devices used) Pen plotters and direct storage view tubes (DVST) devices are used. Cathode ray tubes (CRT) are used.
4. (Cost) The cost of device used for Random scan is much higher The cost of device used for Raster scan is much cheaper.
5. (Memory Requirement) Requires less memory Requires more memory
6. (Other requirements) Requires Intelligent electron beam i.e process controlled No such requirements
