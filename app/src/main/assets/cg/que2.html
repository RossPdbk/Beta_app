Q2.Explain what is computer graphics and its applications?

Computer graphics:

Computer graphics is a study of techniques to improve communication between human and machine

Applications:

  • Engineering and scientific software,Business software.
  • T.V. channels , space simulation training.
  • PCB designing and map preparation.
  • User interface , animation.
  • Making charts , image processing.
  • Office automation.
  • Desktop publishing.
  • CAD/CAM
  • Visual effects in movies and computer games.
