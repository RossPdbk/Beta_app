Steps for midpoint circle algorithm are as follows:-

  1. Accept radius 'r' and center of circle (Xc,Yc) from user and plot first point on circumference of circle .
    (X0,Y0)=(0,r)
  2. Calculate the initial value of the decision parameter.
    P0=1-r
  3. If we are using octant symmetry property to plot the pixels ,then until (X If Pk <0
    modify Pk as Pk=Pk+2X+1 and then increase X by 1 ie X++
    otherwise
    modify Pk as Pk= Pk+2(X-Y)+1 and increase X by 1 and decrease Y by 1
  4. Determine the symmetry point in other octant also.
  5. calculate all points as
    X=X+Xc and
    Y=Y+Yc
