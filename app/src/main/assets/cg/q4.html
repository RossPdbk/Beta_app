Q4.Explain DDA line drawing algorithm and write its advantages and disadvantages?

Digital differential analyzer (DDA) is based on incremental method.
Steps for DDA algorithm are as follows
  1.  Accept two end points . Say (X a,Ya) and (X b ,Y b)
  2.  Find out horizontal and vertical difference between end points
    Dx=Xb-Xa and Dy=Yb-Ya
  3.  Difference between greater magnitude determines the value of parameter steps.
  4.  Determine the offset needed at each step.
  5.   If |Dx|> |Dy| and Xa < X b , then the value of the increment in the X and Y direction are 1 and m respectively.
  6.   If |Dx|>|Dy| and Xa > X b , then the decrement -1 and -m are used to generate new point .
  7.   similarly if |Dx|<|Dy| and Y a < Yb then the values of increments in X and Y direction are 1/m and 1 respectively.
  8.   Similarly if |Dx|<|Dy| and Y a > Y b ,then the values of increments in X and Y - direction are -1/m and -1 .
  •   Advantages:-
  1.  The DDA algorithm is a faster method for calculating pixel positions than the direct use of line equation y=md+b
  2.  It is very easy to understand.
  3.  It requires no special skills for implementation
  •   Disadvantages:-
  1.  Because of round off,errors are introduced and causes the calculated position to drift away from the true line path.
  2.  Because of floating point operations the algorithm is time-consuming.
