Q25. What are differences between Deadlock Prevention and Deadlock avoidance.

No. Deadlock Prevention Deadlock avoidance
1. In deadlock prevention, We ensure that any of four necessary conditions dows not occur. In deadlock avoidance, System needs additional information about how resources are to be requested and by knowing we try to avoid deadlock condition.
2. It does not need knowledge of future resource request. It requires all knowledge of all resource request at the beginning itself.
3. Device utilization is low and throughput is reduced. device utilization is high and throughput is increased as compared to prevention.
4. Major drawback is degradation of system efficiency. Major drawback is that it is difficult to have information of all resources needed at very first stage of process.
5. It includes non-blocking synchro algorithm, dijkstra algorithm, serializing tokens etc. For single instance of resource type --> Resouce allocation graph & For multiple instance type --> Banker's algorithm.
6. Preemtion happens. No preemption.
