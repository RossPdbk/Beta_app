Write short note on Scanner.

  1. A scanner is an input device that scans documents such as photographs and pages of text.
  2. When a document is scanned, it is converted into a digital format.
  3. The two main types of scanners are flatbed and portable scanners.
  4. A flatbed scanner is the most common type of scanner, and it is designed to scan flat objects.
  5. A portable scanner is designed for travel purposes.
  6. Most flatbed scanners have a cover that lifts up so that books and other bulky objects can also be scanned.
  7. While sheet-fed scanner, which can only accept paper documents.
  8. Optical scanners capture the image of a usually flat object and transfer it to a computer, much like flatbed scanners. In order to produce a better quality image, as most people strive for in their printing, you need a higher resolution scanner.
  9. The resolution of a scanner is measured in dots per 12-inches, which makes sense because the more dots you have, the more color that shows up, producing higher quality scans.
  10. Scanners usually attach to your personal computer with a Small Computer System Interface ( SCSI ).
